    Urgent: Beware of Perbadanan Tabung Pendidikan Tinggi Nasional (PTPTN) Loan Scam Calls and Fake Links

    Dear Students,

    We have been alerted to a fraudulent scheme targeting PTPTN loan recipients. Scammers are calling students, claiming to be from PTPTN, and then sending fake links, urging recipients to click and provide personal details such as their ID and bank account information.

    Important Reminders:
    • PTPTN will never ask for your personal or banking details via phone calls, SMS, or unofficial links.
    • Do not click on any suspicious links or follow instructions from unknown sources.
    • If you receive such a call or message, report it immediately to PTPTN or the relevant authorities.

    For official PTPTN updates, always refer to their official website (www.ptptn.gov.my) or contact them directly through verified channels.

    If you need further clarification or assistance, you may also contact the Government and Corporate Liaison Unit of the Registry at registry-gclu@sunway.edu.my

    Stay vigilant and protect your personal information.

    Registry

    Announcement : Dean's List Award

    Dear Students,

    We are pleased to announce that starting with the April 2024 semester, the University will honour students with outstanding academic performance by awarding them the Dean's List Award. The award will be given based on the following criteria:

    • Degree Types: MQF Level 4 Diploma (including Diploma in Nursing) and MQF Level 6 Bachelor's Degree (including students in the ADTP programme).
    • Semester Awarded: Students will be awarded across all semesters throughout the duration of the programme study.
    • Grade Point Average (GPA): The student will need to achieve a minimum GPA of 3.50 for the given semester.
    • Minimum Credits: The minimum credits that contribute to the GPA for a given semester are:
      • Long Semester: 12 credits
      • Short Semester: 6 credits
    • No Failing Grades: Students will not be eligible for the Dean's List award if they have any failing grades for the given semester.
    • Academic Integrity: Students will not be eligible for the Dean's List award if they committed any academic malpractice or misconduct during the semester.
    We look forward to acknowledging and honouring the exceptional efforts and commitment of our students. Best wishes to those who will achieve this distinguished recognition.
    NOTICE: Let's Build a Respectful Community Together

    Dear Students,

    As part of our commitment to maintaining a respectful and cooperative relationship within the Sunway community, we would like to highlight an important matter concerning the Sunway Medical Centre (SMC). The hospital has raised concerns about students loitering on their premises, especially after visiting hours. While we understand that the hospital's proximity to BRT and campus makes it a convenient waiting area, it is essential to respect their policies and ensure the environment required for patient care and comfort.

    We kindly remind you to refrain from loitering at SMC, particularly after visiting hours, and to make full use of the facilities available on campus for discussions, study sessions, or simply as a place to hang out with friends.

    Additionally, we would like to remind all students that adherence to the Student Code of Conduct (SCC) is expected. Any breach of the SCC may result in disciplinary action. We urge you to familiarize yourself with the guidelines outlined in the SCC (link provided below) and conduct yourselves accordingly.
