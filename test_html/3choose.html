Enrolment

Dear Students,

1. The iZone online subject enrolment will open on Thursday, 10 April 2025 at 12:00pm and will closes on
Wednesday, 16 April 2025 at 11:59pm.

2. The iZone online enrolment session will be unavailable daily for system updates during the following
times:
• 12.10am – 1.10am
• 2.10pm – 3.10pm

3. Please refer to the following guide on how to perform online enrolment of subjects via iZone:
iZone Online Enrolment Student Guide.pdf

4. Please ensure that you enrol in the lecture, tutorial, practical, and workshop groups assigned to your
programme and intake. Once you click the submit button, you will be able to view your timetable on iZone.
The school reserves the right to reject or amend the groupings if you fail to enroll according to your
timetable.

5. For guidance in selecting your elective subjects, please refer to the Elective Subject Trailers and Slides
folder located on the left-hand panel of CIS eLearn.

6. If you are unable to view a subject in iZone, please check whether you have fulfilled the pre-requisite(s) for
that subject.
