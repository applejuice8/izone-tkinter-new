First Time Password Activation Instruction:

1
 What is my Username and First Time Password?

With effect from 3 March 2015, your Username will be your Student ID. Your First Time Password is in this format (YYYYMmmXXXX).

YYYY is your 4-digit year of birth.
Mmm is the 3-letter abbreviation of your birth month with the first letter in capital.
XXXX is the last 4 characters of your IC No. (for Malaysian) or passport No. (for non-Malaysian).

Example (for Malaysian): If your birth date is 31 August 1999 and your IC No. is 990831-12-7887, your password will be - 1999Aug7887.

Example (for non-Malaysia): If your birth date is 01 January 1999 and your Passport No. is A9876540, your password will be - 1999Jan6540.
2
 If you have not changed your First Time Password before, you will need to follow the steps below before you can login to iZone:


Click on "First Time Password Activation" in iZone's login page and login with your Username and First Time Password.

You will be guided to select 4 challenge questions for future password self reset service.

Your new password must contain at least eight (8) characters and no more than sixteen (16) characters with a combination of:

  • Alphanumeric (a to z and 0 to 9)
  • Upper and lowercase (A and a)
  • Special characters (!@#$%&)
The followings are prohibited from being used in your new password:

  • Blank space
  • The same password
  • Part of your name or username
  • Dictionary words
You must NOT reveal your password to anyone in anyway.
